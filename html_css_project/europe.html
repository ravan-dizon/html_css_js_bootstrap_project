WELCOME TO EUROPE

A Grand Tour of Europe is an iconic trip foe a reason: The content packs an incredible amount of diversity into a relatively small space. Feast on pasta and gelato in Italy, chase the Northern Lights in Scandinavia, and island hop in Greece. Or step off-the-beaten-path to see Romania's haunting castles and the Baltics charming seaside.

1.England

Explore historical-turned-trendsetting London marvel at the stone circle at Stonehenge, be a part of Beatlemania in Liverpool, or hit the beach in Brighton- here's what to do in England

2. France

It's no surprise that France welcomes more tourist than any other country in the world. If you're dreaming of never-ending baguettes, colorful macarons, and buttery almonds croissants, read on.

3.Ukraine

Step off the beaten track in kiev, an affordable capital city with a sparkling skyline.

4. Germany

Berlin's important 20th-century history, Bavarias charming castles, and Munich's Oktoberfest celebration should put this country high on your must-see list.

5. Ireland

The green countryside, lively puds, and friendly residents make Ireland a perpetual favorite for visitors. Farm-fresh cuisines and Guinness from the source only sweeten the deal.

6. Italy

The mozzarella alone could alone justify the cost of airfare. Add in maze-like medieval hill towns, wine thats's cheaper than water, and art more famous than the Pope..and, well are you still doing at home?

7. Netherlands

Co Dutch in the land of whimsical windmills, winding mazes of waterways, and enchanting fields of colorful tulips.

8. Spain

SSpain is vibrant and diverse. Embrace your inner art nerd at El Prado in Madrid, stroll the busting boardwalk along the Mediterranean in Barcelona, and have some of the wildest nights of your life in libiza.

9. Switzerland

The country's daily routine runs like clockwork, it's gooey raclette cheese is to die for, and its Alps are jaw-dropping in every season.

10.Norway

Experience the Northern Lights and the Midnight Sun in the country that was recently named the happiest in the world.
