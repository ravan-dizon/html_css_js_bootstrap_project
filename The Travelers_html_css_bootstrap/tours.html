COFFEE-LICIUOS

 At The Coffee-Liciuos our standard and number one priority is quality. This is why our coffee master, selects only the top 1% Arabica beans that the world has to offer from Latin America, East Africa, and the Pacific.
The origin of our beans matters to us, so we source from and partner with small farms and private estates from the top-tier growing regions of the world. This is to ensure that what we use is only of the finest caliber, handpicked by some of the most prestigious growers on earth.
