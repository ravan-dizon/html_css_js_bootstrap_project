Welcome to Bolinao

Bolinao Tour Package. The famous Hundred Islands National Park, located in the province of Pangasinan has four famous Islands: Quezon Island, Marcos Island, Lopez Island and Children’s Island.The Hundred Islands is home to many caves and terrains that are suitable for hiking and camping trips. Enjoy the picturesque view through various viewing decks in the islands. With our very affordable Bolinao Tour Package, you can now experience the complete island experience!
