Welcome to Baguio

Baguio City, the Summer Capital of the Philippines, is famous for its cold climate, tourist spots, pine trees, crafts and delicacies. Every place in Baguio has its own story to tell—from the historical Baguio Cathedral, Mines View Park, Burnham Park, Camp John Hay, The Manor, Philippine Military Academy, famous streets and more.Experience the best of the Summer Capital of the Philippines with its cold climate, and you’ll have the rest and fun you need. Enjoy our Baguio Tour Package and see the finest spots that tourists from all over the world visit every day!
