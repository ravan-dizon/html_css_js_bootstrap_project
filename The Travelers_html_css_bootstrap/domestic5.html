Welcome to bohol

Bohol is located in the heart of Central Visayas with a narrow strait that separates it to the equally famous city of Cebu. Bohol is famous for its Chocolate Hills(a group of unusually shaped of hills covered with grass that turns brown like chocolate), Philippine tarsier(one of the smallest primates and indigenous in the island) and white sand beaches (in Anda and Panglao Area). Also for the adventurous at heart, you could avail their other activities like ziplining, caving, kayaking and etc.
