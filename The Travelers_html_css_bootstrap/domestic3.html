Welcome to Ilocos

Ilocos tour packages 3 days and 2 nights. Travel back in time with our very affordable Ilocos tour packages 3 days and 2 nights and enjoy Pagudpod and Vigan. Ilocos is known for Vigan City’s rich historical past and paradise beaches and windmills in Pagudpod.

Enjoy the 16th century feel of the churches, the mansions, museums, and the markets. Dive and swim in Ilocos’ beaches. Have fun interacting with the hospitable people of Ilocos with our Ilocos Package Tour Plus Vigan.

In Pagudpod plus Vigan Tour Package, feast and be merry with Ilocos’ local delicacies like pinakbet, empanada, longganisa, and chichacorn.
